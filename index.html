Aqua Gaming is a gaming organization that uses 50% of its funds gained from selling game titles to organizations aimed to save our sea wildlife. This website will be used to promote games that will provide our organization with money in order for it to be donated so we can save our oceans.

Aqua Gaming wishes to one day clean up all of our precious oceans. Our mission is to clean and preserve 15% of all sea life by 2040. With your support we can save the remaining sea life!
