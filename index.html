About us!

Aqua Gaming is a gaming organization that uses 50% of its funds gained from selling game titles to organizations aimed to save our sea wildlife. This website will be used to promote games that will provide our organization with money in order for it to be donated so we can save our oceans.

Why shop with us?

Aqua Gaming knows how much you like the oceans and gaming. By buying your favorite games through us, a percentage of your purchase will be donated to organisations that help protect and restore our oceans. To learn more about the prganisations we support head to the About us! section.
